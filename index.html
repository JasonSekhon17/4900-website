Services We Provide

Wash and Fold offers the highest quality services for supreme results. Our services include washing, folding and delivery . One simple laundering facility to make life easier in present day, our lives are filled with chaos and various tedious tasks leaving us with little to no time with our family or even ourselves. Wash and Fold understands our clients’ needs to unwind, and takes an initiative to free you from your laundering worries. An experienced staff, high-end equipment, best quality cleaning agents and a result-based approach makes it all possible for us to stand by our promise.

This is our basic services which involves just washing, drying and folding your laundry and returned back to you within 48 hours of picking up. Your items are shrink wrapped after folding and you can choose to have us put them on hangers. This services is billed by the pound or load depending on your location.

You can have all or some of your items dry cleaned as part of the wash, dry and fold services you get on a regular basis or you could just order only dry cleaning where available. The prices vary based on the items being dry cleaned.

Don't waste your day doing laundry—make the most of it with our Load & Leave Service! Simply put your laundry in the washer, start the machine and let our laundry service associate know that you would like our Load & Leave Service. We'll transfer your laundry to the dryer, fold it and call you when it's done. It's that simple! Head to the mall, get your grocery shopping done, wash your car or grab a bite to eat.
